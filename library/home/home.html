Online library

An online library is a digital platform that provides access to a vast collection of books, journals, articles, and other educational resources over the internet. Users can browse, borrow, or read content from anywhere with an internet connection, often for free or with a subscription. Online libraries are convenient, offering resources in various formats such as eBooks, audiobooks, and PDFs. They serve as valuable tools for learning, research, and entertainment, allowing users to access a wide range of materials at any time. Examples include platforms like Google Books, Project Gutenberg, and online academic libraries.
